About Mike

Hi, I'm Mike—your friendly neighborhood detectorist with over a decade of experience sweeping beaches, parks, and historical sites across the country. This site is my digital dig log where I share stories, tips, and gear reviews to help fellow treasure hunters make their next great find.

Pro Tips & Tricks

  • Start slow and overlap your swings for full ground coverage.
  • Always dig a proper plug to leave no trace behind.
  • Research local history to increase your odds of finding something interesting.
  • Use headphones to pick up faint signals others might miss.
  • Double‑check your holes—sometimes treasure hides twice!
